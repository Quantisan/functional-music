             Hotel California Live Electric - The Eagles
----------------------------------------------------------------------------
Tabbed by:Adam Metcalfe
Email:atm.8@hotmail.com

Tuning:eBGDAE



So I looked everywhere for the electric version of
this live.  I couldn't find it anywhere so I decided
I'd try and figure it out on my own.  One of these days
I'll do a video tutorial to better explain what all is
going on here in the tab.  Walsh's part might not be perfect 
but it's close.  Keep in mind this is NOT the acoustic version 
from Hell Freezes Over which has many tabs of it both on UG and 
other sites.  This was tabbed from the version on Eagles Live (1980),
from video of their 1977 Houston concert, and the music video.

You'll have to listen to the song to get the note values and
timing, this tab is tedious enough without worrying about them

There are 3 guitars, they are as follows:

12-string acoustic w/ capo on 2nd fret*: Glenn Frey

6 string electric (Fender Telecaster if you care): Joe Walsh

Gibson double-neck w/ capo on 7th fret of 12-string neck: Don Felder

*If you watch the video Frey has a capo on the second fret,
but his chord fingerings are as they would be in standard
tuning.  I think he just has the guitar tuned down a step
and uses the capo to bring it to "standard" which was a 
common thing to do with older 12-strings.  Anyways, in the 
video from the Hotel California tour he has a capo; ignore it.


This part might be wrong but it works.  It's heavily palm muted and
